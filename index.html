Freedom is an illusion.

Freedom is often seen as the ultimate goal, but it can also be a trap. We may think we are free, but in reality, we are often bound by our own desires and expectations. The more we seek freedom, the more we may find ourselves imprisoned by our own desires and expectations. In the end, freedom is not about having no restrictions, but about finding a balance between our desires and the reality of our situation. We must learn to accept the limitations of our freedom and find peace within them.
